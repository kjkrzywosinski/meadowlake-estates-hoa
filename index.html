About Meadow Lake Estates

Meadow Lake Estates is a family-friendly neighborhood located in Bay City, Michigan, near Bay Valley Golf Course. The community features a serene environment with scenic views, making it an ideal place for families and individuals seeking a peaceful lifestyle. The neighborhood is known for its spacious lots, allowing residents to enjoy outdoor activities.

Our Homeowners Association (HOA) is dedicated to maintaining the quality and integrity of the neighborhood through careful planning, community events, and regular maintenance of issues or disputes that may arise.

Close to I-75 and minutes from Downtown Bay City, Saginaw and Midland. HOA Fee $150 a year. All building/renovation plans must be approved by Meadow Lakes HOA.
